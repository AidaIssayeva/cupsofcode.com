Privacy Policy for `Duplicates` Add-On

Jul 14, 2023 1 min read

Last updated: July 14th, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Duplicates handles and protects user information while using the add-on. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.
